Uncle Frank's Famous Flapjacks

Description

These peanut butter & chocolate chip flapjacks are packed full of peanut flavour and energy, perfect for refuelling during your epic summer adventuring. No mixing bowl needed!

    Ingredients

  • 1 cup butter, melted
  • 2/3 cup brown sugar
  • 4 tbsp golden syrup
  • 1/2 cup peanut butter
  • 450 g porridge oats
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 cup milk chocolate chips

Method

  1. Heat butter, brown sugar and golden syrup in a large saucepan over a gentle heat.
  2. Stir in the peanut butter when the butter has completely melted.
  3. Remove from the heat, add the vanilla and the porridge oats. Cool
  4. Stir in the chocolate chips.
  5. Spoon into a baking tin lined with non-stick paper. Smooth over the top.
  6. Bake at 350°F/ 170°C fan for 30 minutes. Cut and cool.
